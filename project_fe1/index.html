Investor– Finance and Investment Joomla Template

Investor is a Modern & Trendy Joomla theme created for investment, finance, accounting, advisory, banking, corporate, business, company, consulting, marketing services.

Investor comes with home page, about us, service, project, blog, shop, contact page, etc… that ensure the fulfillment of your needs. It is fully responsive so it looks beautiful at any size, be it a laptop screen, iPad, iPhone, Android Mobile or tablets.

It also comes with extensive documentation and support by our top notch team. Support part is the most important thing for us. We always try our best to serve customers and make sure they are happy with our product.

SP Page builder included ($49 saved)

Investor is built with fantastic SP Page Builder. It is easy to use and also easy to edit. Only with drag and drop you can create complex layouts. With SP Page Builder you can easily customize your website just in a few clicks.

T3 Framework(100% Responsive)

Investor built with the best Joomla Framework – T3, the template is easy to use with initutive Admin control panel that has a lot of options and tool to manage your site. T3 built with Bootstrap so the template looks perfect on all devices and screen sizes like Mobile,Tablet and Desktop.

K2 Blog Pages

Investor comes with K2 blog pages . It already has various features so you have a choice to choose what suits you.

J2Store Shopping

Create articles and turn them into products. The template features J2Store, a simple and powerful Joomla shopping cart that lets you turn articles into products. Start selling your products within a few minutes.
